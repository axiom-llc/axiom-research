



Personal AI workers (agents): Soon, individuals will have thousands of personal AI agents to assist them in various tasks.



Empowered individuals: With these AI workers, a single person will have the capability to perform the work equivalent to that of a 10,000-person corporation.



Limitless potential: Over time, there will be no practical limit to the scale at which individuals can operate with AI support.



Massive scale of operations: A single person could potentially manage and coordinate millions of AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) workers, effectively operating at a national scale.
